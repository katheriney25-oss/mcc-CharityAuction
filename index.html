Auction Rules

How Bidding Works

  • Bids must be whole-dollar amounts.
  • Each new bid must be at least $1 higher than the current high bid.
  • The highest bid at auction close wins the item.
  • Winning bidders will be contacted after the auction closes.
  • Payments will be processed directly by Boys & Girls Club.
  • Items must be picked up at the convention unless other arrangements are approved.
